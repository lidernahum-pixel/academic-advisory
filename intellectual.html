Practice · No. 03 Intellectual Property & Licensing

Proprietary frameworks, licensed with care.

For Trusted partners & affiliates

The Academic Advisory licenses proprietary intellectual property — frameworks refined over years of mentorship and advisement — to trusted partners and affiliates, through structured agreements designed to protect the integrity of the work.

02 / How It Works

A considered path to licensure.

01

Inquiry & Introduction

Prospective partners begin with a private conversation. We listen for fit before we discuss frameworks.

02

Review of Proprietary Materials

Under appropriate confidentiality, we walk qualified affiliates through the relevant frameworks and their intended use.

03

Structured Licensing Agreement

Terms are written to protect the integrity of the work and to give partners clear, durable rights to apply it.

04

Training & Onboarding

Licensees are oriented to the philosophy behind the materials — not merely the mechanics of their delivery.

05

Ongoing Stewardship

We remain available to advise affiliates as they put the work into practice and as the frameworks themselves evolve.

06

Renewal & Refinement

Agreements are reviewed periodically so that both the materials and the partnership stay current with the field.
